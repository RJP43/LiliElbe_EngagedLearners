Guide to Proofing

The following is a guide to our style coding for proofing of people, places, idiosyncratic spelling, and Editor/Translator comments. This color coding is intended to remind proofers to mouse over these particular parts of the text while proofing. Please mouse-over every person and place, with the Key ID styling, to verify the key ID matches the information available in the LEDA_Prosopography.xml.

Transcription / Translation

(g01) Aussig, den 09.03.32

(g02) Ich las eine Besprechung Ihres Buches „Fra Mann til Kvinde“ und würde es gerne ins Deutsche übersetzen..

(g03) Bitte schreiben sie mir, ob und zu welchen Bedingungen Sie mir das Übersetzungsrecht überlassen wollen.

(g04) Hochachtungsvoll,
Herta Keller

(#g01) Aussig, the 03/09/32

(#g02) I read a review of your book "Fra Mann til Kvinde" and I would like to translate it into German.

(#g03) Please let me know, if and under what conditions you would authorize this translation.

(#g04) Kind regards,
Herta Keller
