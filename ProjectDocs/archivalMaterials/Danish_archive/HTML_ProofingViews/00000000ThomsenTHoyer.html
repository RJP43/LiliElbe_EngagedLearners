Guide to Proofing

The following is a guide to our style coding for proofing of people, places, idiosyncratic spelling, and Editor/Translator comments. This color coding is intended to remind proofers to mouse over these particular parts of the text while proofing. Please mouse-over every person and place, with the Key ID styling, to verify the key ID matches the information available in the LEDA_Prosopography.xml.

The paper is folded into fourths. The name Thomsen is written in the upper left corner in what appears to be pencil. There are two hole punches on the left margin of the page. There appears to be what looks like an imprint of a logo or picture on the page itself underneath the writing in very light ink.

Transcription / Translation

(d01) Efter Aftale med Fru Lili Elvenes sender jeg Dem hermed et Billede (som jeg gerne ønsker retur, da det er det eneste, jeg ejer) af min Svoger Kunstmaler Einar Wegener.

(d02) Jeg beder Dem tale med mig inden De sender noget uddrag af Manuskriptet til Tyskland – min Telefon-@Translator: kj er efter 6.30 Gentofte 144, og i Løbet af Dagen er det : Central 4726.

(d03) Med megen Agtelse
TC Thomsen

(d04) Ingeniør

(#d01) As agreed with Mrs. Lili Elvenes I hereby send you a picture (which I would like you to return as it is the only one I own) of my brother-in-law painter Einar Wegener.

(#d02) I ask you to talk to me before sending any transcript of the manuscript to Germany – my telephone number after 6:30 is Gentofte 144, and during the day it is : Central 4726.

(#d03) With much respect
TC Thomsen

(#d04) Engineer
