# Lili Elbe Digital Archive Encoding Guidelines  
  
_Note: These guidelines define the Lili Elbe digital archive's encoding expectations and customizations of the TEI guidelines as defined in [the project's ODD file](https://gitlab.com/ctsdh/lili-elbe-code/blob/master/schema/LEDA_ODD.ODD). These guidelines focus on the encoding of the TEI `<text>` element; therefore, encoders should reference [the project's TEI Header template](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/RJP43/LiliElbe_EngagedLearners/master/ProjectDocs/TEIHeader_Template.xml) for encoding expectations/customizations regarding the `<teiHeader>` element._
